- Pricing and price-sensitivity analysis

- Behavioral segmentation and profiling
- Neuromarketing studies using eye-tracking, EEG, facial coding, and implicit measures

### Methods
- Large-scale survey research
- Behavioral data analysis
- Qualitative interviews and thematic coding
- Psychophysiological measurement and analysis
- Automated dashboards and reporting systems
