## Professional Background

I am a **Senior Market Research Specialist** at **Snapp! Box**, with over six years of professional experience at the intersection of **market research, consumer neuroscience, and neuromarketing**. My work focuses on understanding consumer behavior by integrating behavioral data, psychophysiological measurements, and advanced analytical methods to support evidence-based business and policy decisions.

Alongside my industry role, I have been actively involved in academic and applied research in **consumer neuroscience**, collaborating with universities, research laboratories, and commercial organizations on more than **50 neuromarketing and consumer research projects**.

---

## Professional Focus

My expertise spans both **commercial market research** and **experimental consumer neuroscience**, with a strong emphasis on methodological rigor and real-world applicability.

- Market and consumer research for digital platforms and services  
- Survey design, experimental design, and advanced quantitative analysis  
- Consumer neuroscience and neuromarketing research  
- Behavioral and psychophysiological data analysis (EEG, eye tracking, GSR, facial expression analysis)  
- Integration of survey, behavioral, and biometric data  
- Research reporting, visualization, and automation
